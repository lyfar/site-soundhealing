Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Sound Healing is designed with privacy at its core. The app runs entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Collection

Sound Healing does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration or sign-in required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No location tracking
  • No device identifiers collected

Local Data Storage

The app stores your preferences and settings locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms:

  • Instrument tuning parameters
  • Audio session history
  • User interface preferences
  • Saved instrument collections

This data never leaves your device and is managed by iOS. It can be deleted at any time by removing the app.

Microphone Access

Sound Healing may request microphone access for optional features like real-time audio analysis. If granted, audio is processed in real-time on your device and never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Motion Data

The app may request access to device motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) for optional gesture-based controls. This data is processed in real-time on your device and never leaves it.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your app settings may be synchronized across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. This data is encrypted and managed by Apple according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

Sound Healing does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
